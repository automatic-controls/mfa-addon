Configure MFA

Please enter an email where you can receive MFA security codes. You may enter phone number emails as well. For example, if your phone number is 555‑123‑4567, and your provider is AT&T, then 5551234567@txt.att.net is appropriate. Verizon phone numbers use the extension @vtext.com instead. You can optionally also configure an authenticator app, which will take precedence; however, an email address is still recommended for a backup login method.
